Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays inside Chrome’s local storage on your machine. Tab Vault never sends your browsing data to external servers.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Saving and restoring sessions works without an internet connection. You only need connectivity when purchasing premium through ExtPay.

How do I upgrade team members?

Use the ExtPay dashboard’s “Add free users” function to grant premium access to specific email addresses—ideal for teams and classrooms.

Can I contribute or report issues?

Absolutely. Visit the GitHub repo to open issues, contribute improvements, or read the roadmap.
